SNUVOICE collects SNU students’ voice. The attempts of SNU students to gather their opinions and share their information through various methods (student council, school media, school organizations) have persisted, but most have been futile and lacking in quality. To solve this problem, SNUVOICE uses two microphones, Tell Me and Hear Us, to gather, deliver, and store the voices and opinions of SNU students in a permanent, accessible form.
Tell Me is a wiki service only for SNU. In Tell Me, you will be able to access any SNU-related information. Freshmen, exchange students, those interested in double major/minor can all visit Tell Me to access the newest, temporary, verified SNU-related information.
Hear Us is a petition service devoted to SNU. Hear Us can gather the opinion of many to create a single, consistent voice that represents the student body. Hear Us also offers statistical data and visual representations of vote results.
